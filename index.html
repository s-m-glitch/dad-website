About

David McKean is the former United States Ambassador to Luxembourg and the Director of Policy Planning at the United States Department of State. He received the Distinguised Honor Award from the Department of State. He is a graduate of Harvard College, The Fletcher School of Law and Diplomacy, and Duke University School of Law. He has authored six books, and his seventh is forthcoming. His writing has appeared in The Washington Post, Foreign Affairs, Foreign Policy, The Boston Globe, USA Today, Politico, Barrons, and many other publications. He divdes his time between Washington DC and New Hampshire with his wife and dog.
